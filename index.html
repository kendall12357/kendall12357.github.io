Fluid Power Simulation

Swashplate Piston Pump Simulation

Simulated a 45 cc/rev swashplate piston pump with 11 pistons to model pump kinematics, flow rate, chamber pressure, Poiseuille and Couette leakages, and volumetric efficiency across varying RPM and swash plate angles.

Built design assumptions from pump geometry, valve plate timing, orifice flow, leakage equations, and fluid properties, then compared performance trends across multiple operating conditions.

Tools: MATLAB, Autodesk Inventor, Fluid Power Modeling, Pump Simulation, Data Visualization

View project PDF

Refinery Analytics

Python/Seeq Heater Flood Prediction

Validated and built Python/Seeq predictive analytics applications to identify approaching heater flood conditions and support proactive operator intervention, process safety, and reliability.

Tools: Python, Seeq, Process Data, Predictive Analytics

Energy & Reliability

Plate Air Preheater Performance Model

Developed an Excel/VBA performance model to monitor heat duty loss, fouling factor, and fuel cost impacts for refinery plate air preheaters, identifying a crude unit APH responsible for approximately $35,000 in annual excess fuel costs.

Tools: Excel, VBA, Heat Transfer, Mechanical Integrity

Manufacturing Automation

Automated Dimensional Measurement Analysis

Automated aerospace casting dimensional analysis with Python and VBA, creating trend reports, Trailing 30 vs. Trailing 60 comparisons, Pareto charts, and airfoil location visualizations that reduced recurring analysis time.

Tools: Python, VBA, Minitab, Excel, Statistical Analysis

CAD & Tooling
